The Roosevelt Suite

The Roosevelt Suite is our exclusive Hunting Lodge located just north of the resort.
The suite comfortably houses up to 20 people, comes with jacuzzi and sauna-room as well as being just a short drive from our hunting grounds.
Located about two miles north-west of the lodge.
The Roosevelt Suite charges $1,000 per night and comes fully stocked with food and drink.

*Additional provisions can be ordered from the central resort and will be delivered within an hour

The Favella

The Roosevelt a bit too rich for your blood? You're not alone.
The Favella is our economy class lodging, but believe us when we say we will not disappoint.
A standard Favella room charges $100 per night

Upon renting your room you will be assigned to one of our eight "Favellas".
Each Favella comes standard with a pool and park area where our staff will host events and games for any children you might have.
And while the kids are busy, another one of our staff members will facilitate some "Adult Activities".
*See the Activities section for further information.
